How it works

The whole interface
is one key.

01

Hold.

Right Alt, from any app. Nothing to launch, nothing to click.

02

Speak.

A small pill listens near your cursor — words stream in live.

03

Release.

Punctuated, capitalized text — in under half a second.

On-device

Nothing leaves
this laptop.

NVIDIA Parakeet 0.6B runs on your CPU via ONNX Runtime. Your voice is decoded in memory and typed at your cursor — that's the entire pipeline.
